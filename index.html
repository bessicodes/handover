Proprietary Systems

Built-in fraud & access controls

Smart-Weight Anti-Fraud Scale

The courier's device weighs the package at pickup and again at drop-off. Any mismatch flags the transaction and freezes escrow before funds move.

SafeHub Lockers

Camera-verified, unattended drop points for recipients who can't meet the courier — the same PIN-release logic applies to the locker door.

Tiered Transaction Insurance

Every handover carries flat R500 cover at no extra cost. Buyers moving higher-value goods can opt into a high-value tier that raises the limit and routes the job to top-rated couriers only.
